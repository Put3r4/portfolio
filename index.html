Data Pipeline · Feature Engineering · EDA

Pantau Pasar — National Food Price
Data Pipeline & Feature Engineering

Role: Data Scientist · Coding Camp powered by DBS Foundation × Dicoding

23,709 rows · National level
456,590 rows · 514 districts/cities
21 food commodities integrated

Data Collection & Cleaning

  • Integrated historical price data for 21 strategic food commodities from PIHPS Nasional and SP2KP Kemendag (Jan 2022 – Apr 2026), spanning 5 Excel files + 1 CSV.
  • Resolved header noise, corrected data types, and performed wide-to-long restructuring for analytical compatibility.
  • Applied per-commodity interpolation for missing values and IQR-based outlier labeling across all commodity columns.
  • Produced a clean national-level dataset of 23,709 rows and a district/city-level dataset of 456,590 rows across 514 administrative regions.

EDA & Correlation Analysis

  • Conducted correlation testing over the 2022–2026 window; identified correlations above 0.95 among commodity variants.
  • Recommended consolidating 21 commodities into 6 strategic groups to reduce redundancy and multicollinearity.

Feature Engineering

  • Engineered 11 external feature columns: calendar features (national holidays, Ramadan flag, days-to-Eid) and daily weather metrics via automated Open-Meteo API integration.
  • Designed advanced preprocessing plan: encoding strategy, lag/rolling window features, and time-based train/test splits to prevent data leakage.

Business Visualizations

  • Year-over-Year price growth analysis across all 21 commodities.
  • Coefficient of variation heatmaps for commodity price volatility.
  • Ramadan seasonal impact analysis on food prices.
  • Rainfall vs. chili & shallot price correlation charts.
